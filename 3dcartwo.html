FORD GT LE MANSORY

In celebration of MANSORY's 30th anniversary in 2020, the "Le MANSORY" emerges as an extraordinary edition, limited to a mere 3 units globally, following the tradition of "One car per decade". This exclusive masterpiece bears the distinct signature of MANSORY's complete vehicle transformations, encapsulating three decades of automotive excellence. Crafted as an homage to the iconic Ford GT40 and its legendary triumph at Le Mans in 1966, the "Le MANSORY" embodies the essence of motorsport prowess. Its compelling presence is immediately apparent, as the extended 5 cm full carbon body showcases a comprehensive aerodynamic overhaul, resonating with racing enthusiasts. The heart of the "Le MANSORY" undergoes a remarkable transformation.
