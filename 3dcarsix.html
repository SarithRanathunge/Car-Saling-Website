FORD GT

Introducing the exhilarating 2022 Ford GT, a true track-focused marvel that combines power, precision, and pure performance. With its striking grey exterior adorned with bold black and orange stripes, this car exudes an aura of aggression and speed that is sure to turn heads wherever you go. The heart of the Ford GT is its twin-turbocharged 3.5-liter V-6 engine, delivering an incredible burst of power reminiscent of its F-150 Raptor sibling. This high-performance engine, combined with advanced aerodynamics and a lightweight carbon fiber construction, allows the GT to slice through the air with utmost precision and corner with unrivaled agility. Step inside the driver-focused cockpit and you'll be greeted by a minimalist interior that puts you squarely in control. Every control and essential function is thoughtfully placed on the race-car-inspired steering wheel, allowing you to keep your focus on the road ahead. The fixed driver's seat covered in Alcantara is expertly engineered, with adjustable pedals and a steering wheel to accommodate your perfect driving position. The digital gauge cluster, inspired by the Ford GT race car, provides vital information at a glance, while the suede-wrapped dashboard houses an additional digital display for infotainment functions.

While the GT prioritizes the thrill of driving, it still offers modern technology features to enhance your experience. The 6.5-inch touchscreen supports Ford's Sync 3 software, providing seamless connectivity and control. Voice commands allow you to effortlessly navigate the system, while built-in navigation ensures you stay on the right track during your exhilarating drives. Carbon fiber takes center stage in the GT's design, with its lightweight yet incredibly strong properties showcased in elements such as side mirrors, rims, and steering wheel. This not only adds to the car's performance but also underscores its commitment to cutting-edge engineering and craftsmanship. Experience the true essence of track-focused performance with the 2022 Ford GT. This extraordinary machine is a testament to Ford's dedication to pushing the boundaries of what's possible on the road and the track.
